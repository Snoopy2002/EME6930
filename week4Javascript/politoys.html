Polistil Corporate History

The corporate history is very well covered here on Hobbytalk, although some of their dates are not quite correct on production lines.
The company started producing plastic models, such as the cream Lancia Flaminia at the top of the photos around 1960,
and in 1965 began a very popular and high quality series in metal,
designated Politoys-M. They were perhaps the first company in the world to mass produce quality diecaste models
in 1:24th/1:25th scale, which became increasingly popular in the 1970s and 1980s led by Burago,
another Italian company. Collectability, particularly of the somewhat more rare M versions and generally rare 1/25th mint boxed models,
has been increasing with associated increase in price. The short video feature here, on the Politoys Abarth 3000, gives an idea of the high level of quality
and detail these models had for their time.
